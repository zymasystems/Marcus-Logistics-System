About Me

Marcus Tiisetso Leso

I am Marcus Tiisetso Leso, born on 09 September 2000. I founded Marcus Logistics & Shuttle Service at the age of 23, based in Polokwane. My mission is to provide safe, luxurious transportation that ensures people can move from point A to point B with comfort and peace of mind.

With a focus on safety, punctuality, and customer satisfaction, I've built a service that prioritizes your needs and delivers exceptional travel experiences.
